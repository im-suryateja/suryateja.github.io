Effects of Privatization on Space Missions

Uncovering the changes occurred to the industry of Space Mission over time as Private industry set foot into the field

  • Analyze the impact of privatization on space missions, focusing on private companies like SpaceX, Blue Origin, and Virgin Galactic.
  • Privatization has brought innovation, cost-efficiency, and increased commercial opportunities, accelerating space exploration.
  • Utilized datasets from The Center for Strategic and International Studies and NASA; used Tableau for visualizations.
  • Key Findings:
    • Decrease in mission failure rates since 2004.
    • Reduced cost per kilogram for space launches.
    • Increased number of space missions, especially post-2016.
