Statewise and Regional Car Price & Mileage Analysis

Project Description

This project uses a real-world dataset of used cars listed across various U.S. regions and states to analyze factors affecting asking price and odometer reading. We applied ANOVA and Tukey’s HSD post-hoc tests to compare means across states, fuel types, vehicle conditions, and regions using R programming.

  • Used Levene’s Test to check for homogeneity of variances across regions.
  • Conducted a one-way ANOVA
  • Post-hoc Tukey HSD analysis
