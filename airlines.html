Airline Data Analysis and Strategic Recommendations

  • The report identifies the top 10 busiest round-trip routes based on 1Q2019 data, providing valuable insights into high-demand markets and potential expansion opportunities for the airline company.
  • Through profitability analysis, the report highlights the top 10 most profitable round trip routes, enabling the airline company to prioritize investment in routes with significant profit potential.
  • By considering profitability and punctuality metrics, the report recommends the top 5 round trip routes for investment, emphasizing routes that offer strong profitability potential while maintaining punctuality, aligning with the airline's brand image.
  • The report conducts a breakeven analysis for each recommended route, offering insights into the financial viability of route investment by determining the number of round trip flights required to recoup upfront airplane costs, aiding in informed decision-making regarding route selection.
